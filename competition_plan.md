# Healthcare Competition Transfer Plan

This plan adapts DoomScrollers' strongest Mini-ITC patterns to a healthcare dataset while directly fixing recurring judge concerns.

## 1) Reuse what worked
- Lead with a crisp problem statement and business risk framing.
- Use guided visuals that call out specific highlights.
- Preserve optimization/ROI thinking to prioritize limited investment.
- Keep structured, professional flow from data -> insights -> recommendations.

## 2) Correct known weaknesses
- Replace rough estimates with traceable, dataset-anchored calculations.
- Add explicit timeline for each recommendation.
- Include risk register (overlap risk, rollout risk, data drift, external validity).
- Justify visualization choice on each slide and improve readability.
- Balance speaker ownership and rehearse eye-contact delivery.

## 3) Competition execution sequence
1. **Rubric decomposition:** turn judging criteria into a checklist and point target by section.
2. **Healthcare framing:** map dataset fields to patient, compliance, operations, and financial outcomes.
3. **Data reliability pass:** assumptions table with evidence and downside if assumption fails.
4. **Insight extraction:** concentration analysis, segment analysis, and trend analysis.
5. **Intervention modeling:** 2–4 actions with cost, impact range, confidence, and ownership.
6. **Roadmap:** 30/60/90 day implementation and KPI tracking plan.
7. **Presentation prep:** Q&A bank focused on estimate derivation, overlap, and scalability.

## 4) Minimum deliverables
- Executive summary (decision memo)
- Methods + assumptions appendix
- Annotated visual pack
- ROI model with sensitivity bands
- Risk and mitigation register
- Q&A defense sheet

## 5) Suggested scoring target
- Proposal & Technical Analysis: 45+/50
- Visualization & Business Insights: 18+/20
- Oral Presentation & Team Collaboration: 18+/20
- Overall Proposal Quality: 9+/10
- **Goal:** 90+
